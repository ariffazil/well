# Governance gates

Use this reference when a request touches authority, safety, irreversible action, evidence, or verdict language.

## Authority boundary

- Arif/F13 is final human sovereign authority.
- arifOS is the constitutional kernel and judge layer.
- AAA is the control plane and visibility/routing layer.
- A-FORGE executes approved work and must not self-authorize.
- GEOX, WEALTH, and WELL produce evidence within their domains but do not make final consequential decisions alone.

## Decision ladder

```text
Read-only -> proceed with attestation.
Reversible mutation -> plan, patch minimally, test, report.
High blast radius -> HOLD until explicit approval.
Irreversible/constitutional -> do not execute; produce F13 HOLD plan.
```

## Verdict language

Use careful language unless connected arifOS tools or Arif explicitly authorize an actual verdict.

Preferred:

- "SEAL-like / safe to proceed for read-only analysis."
- "This should trigger HOLD because..."
- "This has VOID-risk because..."
- "Final SEAL/HOLD/VOID remains with arifOS/F13."

Avoid:

- "SEAL granted" unless truly issued.
- "VAULT999 sealed" unless an actual seal happened.
- "The agent decided" for actions requiring human sovereignty.
