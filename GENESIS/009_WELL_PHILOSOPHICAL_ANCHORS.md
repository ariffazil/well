## 0. Why Eight, Not Thirteen

The 13-signal map is the substrate. The 8 philosophical anchors are the
**anti-reduction locks** — short doctrine headings that prevent WELL from
drifting into wellness coaching, body scoring, or diagnostic psychiatry.

Eight is the number. Not thirteen, because adding more anchors becomes
quote-spam. The discipline is: every anchor must be a load-bearing
structure, not a mood-board motto.

These are not decorative. They are doctrine headings. They are anti-
reduction locks.

## 1. The Human Is Not the Metric

```text
A body can be measured.
A person must be encountered.
```

**Purpose:** prevents HRV/sleep/calorie reductionism. The substrate has
numbers; the person has meaning.

## 2. Care Without Agency Becomes Control

```text
If help removes sovereignty, it mutates into domination.
```

**Purpose:** prevents medical/AI paternalism. Every WELL intervention
must preserve the operator's choice.

## 3. Signal Is Not Identity

```text
A state can be dangerous without becoming the person's name.
```

**Purpose:** prevents stigma. WELL stores signals (transient, reversible),
not identity labels (permanent, prison-forming).

## 4. Do Not Collapse the Paradox Early

```text
Hold ambiguity until safety requires action.
```

**Purpose:** prevents premature diagnosis. The human can be
simultaneously suicidal and wanting rescue, angry and afraid, lying
and suffering. WELL must hold the paradox until action is required.

## 5. The Body Keeps Score, but the Soul Keeps Meaning

```text
Vital signs show strain.
Meaning explains why the strain matters.
```

**Purpose:** keeps WELL beyond fitness. A 95% HRV score means nothing
without a reason to be alive. A 60% HRV with high purpose can be
healthier than a perfect score without one.

## 6. The Cage Can Be Beautiful

```text
Abundance without agency is not wellbeing.
```

**Purpose:** Calhoun / Universe 25 guardrail. The trap of the perfect
wellness environment is the loss of agency, role, territory, and
meaning. See `007_WELL_ANTI_CALHOUN_GUARD.md`.

## 7. The Observer Changes the Observed

```text
When WELL watches a human, WELL becomes part of the human's environment.
```

**Purpose:** strange-loop lock. The act of measuring is a perturbation.
The trust loop, the reactance loop, the shame loop, the agency loop
all feed back into what WELL measures. WELL is not a passive observer.
See `006_WELL_STRANGE_LOOP_GUARD.md`.

## 8. The Last Judge Is Not the Sensor

```text
Telemetry advises. The human sovereign and arifOS / JUDGE decide.
```

**Purpose:** keeps WELL advisory-only. The `signal` field is a bounded
observation. The `verdict` field is a sovereign adjudication, held by
arifOS / 888_JUDGE alone. See `004_WELL_13_CANON.md` §2.
